Hi Brain-Teasers :D

This Game improves your Arithmetic skills, Short-term Memory, ability to work under preasure, and Attention.

How to Play :

The aim of the Game is to Make a calculation on 5 numbers. For example Add ( + ).
Your start point will show up when you click on the desired Calculation.
You have to click on the squares one at a time to get a number.
You can't click on the same square twice, nor click on more than 5 squares, or it'll be "Game over".
You submit your answer after the Fifth number shows up.

Enjoy Teasing your brain...
